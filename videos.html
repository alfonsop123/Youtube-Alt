List of Commands

Command Name Usage About
Add Money /add-money (user) (amount) Adds Money to a user
Remove Money /remove-money (user) (amount) Removes Money from a user
Daily /daily Gives you money every 24 hours
Weekly /weekly Gives you money every week
Balance /balance {user} Check your balance
Deposit /deposit (amount) Deposit your money into the bank
Withdraw /withdraw (amount) Withdraw your money from the bank
Work /work Work and earn money every 40 minutes
Crime /crime Commit a crime and receive money or lose money
Rob /rob (user) Rob a user to earn money or lose money
